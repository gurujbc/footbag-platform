These are the official Consecutive Kicks World Records, all of which
were sanctioned by The World Footbag Association:

Women’s Singles: 24,713 – Constance Constable
Open Singles: 63,326 – Ted Martin
Women’s Singles Five-Minute Timed: 804 – Ida Fogle
Open Singles Five-Minute Timed: 1019 – Andy Linder
Women’s Doubles: 34,543 – Constance Constable/Tricia George
Open Doubles: 132,011 – Gary Lautt/Tricia George
Women’s Doubles One-Pass: 6136 – Constance Constable/Tricia George
Open Doubles One-Pass: 27,956 – Ted Martin/Andy Linder
Women’s Doubles Distance One-Pass: 249 – Tricia George/Jody Welch
Open Doubles Distance One-Pass: 622 – Ted Martin/Andy Linder
Open Doubles Ten-Minute Timed One-Pass: 1415 – Tricia George/Paul Vorvick
Open Doubles One-Up-Five-Down: 23,251 – Ted Martin/Andy Linder

The above was copied and pasted from The Footbag Hall of Fame website.
